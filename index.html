BREEDS IN OUR SHED

Holstein

Characteristics: Holstein cows are large in size, weighing about 1,500 pounds in maturity.

History: The Holstein cow originated from the Netherlands, and was first imported to Kenya in the early 1900s.

Jersey

Characteristics: These are the types of cows which are typically light to medium brown in color,

though this can range from being almost grey to dull black. She is small in size, weighing about 1,000 pounds in

maturity. She has a broad face with prominent eyes and long eyelashes which endears this breed to many people.

History:The Jersey cow originated from the Island of Jersey, a small British Island in the English Channel.

Guernnsey

Characteristics: The Guernsey types of cows are brown or fawn with white spots of various sizes on the body or face.

She is medium in size, weighing about 1,200 pounds in maturity.

History: The Guernsey originated from the Isle of Guernsey, a tiny island in the English Channel.
